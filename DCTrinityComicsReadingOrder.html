Superman

Superman was introduced in 1938 and was one of the first superheroes in comic book history. He became so widely successful that he created an entire subgenre of fiction. People tend to write him off as a boring character, but his best stories are full of fun, whimsy, action and hope. This is a list of essential Superman stories to read if you want to get to know the character.
